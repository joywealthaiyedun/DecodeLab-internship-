# SQL Data Analysis Report

## Objective

The objective of this project was to analyze an e-commerce sales dataset using SQL and generate business insights through data querying, aggregation, and trend analysis.

## SQL Skills Applied

* SELECT
* WHERE
* ORDER BY
* GROUP BY
* HAVING
* COUNT()
* SUM()
* AVG()

## Data Quality Validation

Before analysis, the dataset was validated to ensure accuracy.

### Findings

* Duplicate Rows: 0
* Duplicate Order IDs: 0
* Missing Values: 309 (CouponCode only)
* Invalid Data Types: 0

The dataset was considered clean and suitable for analysis.

## Key Findings

### Revenue Performance

* Total Revenue: **$1,264,761.96**
* Total Orders: **1,200**
* Average Order Value: **$1,053.97**

### Product Performance

Top revenue-generating products:

1. Chair
2. Printer
3. Laptop

These products contributed the highest share of total revenue and represent the strongest-performing categories.

### Marketing Performance

Instagram generated the highest number of orders, making it the most effective customer acquisition channel in the dataset.

### Coupon Analysis

The **FREESHIP** coupon generated the highest revenue among all promotional campaigns, indicating that customers respond strongly to free-shipping incentives.

### Payment Analysis

Online payments recorded the highest transaction volume, demonstrating a preference for digital payment methods.

### Operational Analysis

Cancelled and returned orders totaled **497 transactions**, representing **41.4%** of all orders.

This was the most significant issue identified during the analysis and may indicate challenges related to product quality, customer expectations, or fulfillment processes.

### Revenue Trend

Revenue declined across the analysis period:

* 2024 vs 2023: **-13.1%**
* 2025 vs 2024: **-51.7%**

This trend suggests the need for further investigation into sales performance and customer retention.

## Recommendations

* Increase marketing efforts on Instagram.
* Continue leveraging free-shipping promotions.
* Prioritize inventory planning for top-performing products.
* Investigate the causes of high cancellation and return rates.
* Monitor revenue trends through interactive dashboards and recurring reporting.

## Conclusion

SQL analysis provided valuable insights into revenue performance, customer behavior, marketing effectiveness, and operational challenges. The findings highlight strong sales opportunities through promotional campaigns and social media marketing while identifying cancellation and return rates as a key area for business improvement.
