# Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) Report

## Objective

The objective of this analysis was to explore the cleaned e-commerce dataset, identify key trends, understand customer behavior, and generate actionable business insights.

## Dataset Overview

* Total Orders: 1,200
* Total Revenue: $1,264,761.96
* Average Order Value: $1,053.97
* Analysis Period: 2023–2025

## Key Findings

### Revenue Performance

The business generated over **$1.26M** in revenue across 1,200 orders, with an average order value of **$1,053.97**.

### Product Analysis

Top revenue-generating products:

1. Chair
2. Printer
3. Laptop

These products contributed the largest share of overall revenue and should remain a priority for inventory planning and promotions.

### Customer Behavior

* Average cart size: 5.4 items
* Coupon usage rate: 74.25%

This suggests customers frequently purchase multiple items and respond positively to promotional offers.

### Marketing Performance

Instagram generated the highest number of orders, making it the most effective customer acquisition channel in the dataset.

### Coupon Effectiveness

The **FREESHIP** coupon generated the highest revenue, indicating that free-shipping incentives are highly effective in driving purchases.

### Operational Performance

Cancelled and returned orders accounted for **41.4%** of all transactions, highlighting a significant operational concern that requires further investigation.

## Recommendations

* Increase marketing investment in Instagram campaigns.
* Continue leveraging free-shipping promotions.
* Focus inventory planning on top-performing products.
* Investigate the causes of high cancellation and return rates.
* Develop dashboards to continuously monitor revenue, product performance, and customer behavior.

## Conclusion

The analysis revealed strong revenue generation and effective marketing performance, particularly through Instagram and free-shipping promotions. However, the high cancellation and return rate represents a key business challenge that should be addressed to improve overall performance.
